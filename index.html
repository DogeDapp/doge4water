Doge 4 Water

Many Dogecoin users have received their first coins from community faucets. A group of us wondered if we could turn the fun of a faucet into an effort to raise moeny and awareness for those without clean drinking water.

The Dogecoin Foundation is working with the wonderful community to help provide water to people in need.

As part of our "Doge 4 Water" campaign, we're hoping to raise 40 million DOGE by "World Water Day" Saturday March 22, 2014 (equivalent of US$50,000).
